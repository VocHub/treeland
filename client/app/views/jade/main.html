What we want to see in the world

The nation moving towards millions new trees beginning to grow and prosper and existing trees flourishing and spreading.

People making a lifelong commitment to nurture, protect and propagate trees they personally grow or ‘adopt’.

People of all ages striving voluntarily with beautiful, like-minded others, to make unbelievably rich and complex natural settings that outlast us.

The development of a forum, a platform for people to share their experiences and joys, ideas and disappointments as well as plants, seeds, trees and know-how.

A thousand flowers blooming and a million people blossoming as a result of engaging with nature.

People committing a tiny part of themselves to a collective that nurtures all and belongs to all and is led by none.
